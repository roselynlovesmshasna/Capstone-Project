How Points Work

Earn Points

Every $100 you spend at checkout earns you 20 points. Points are added to your balance automatically after payment.

Redeem as Cash

200 points = $1.00 off your order. Apply any amount of points at checkout to reduce your total — points are deducted from your balance immediately after use.

50% Discount Milestone

Collect 1,000 points and unlock a 50% discount on your next order. The 1,000 points are deducted from your balance when the discount is applied.

Points Deduction

Points used as cash or for the milestone discount are removed from your balance after the order is confirmed. Unused points never expire.
